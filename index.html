Who We Are

Founded in 2016, TOPSOFT INTERNATIONAL is an international academic organization affiliated with TOPSOFT group, which aims to embrace the global academic freedom community, as an international collection we very much respect, as well as appreciate the academic freedom, in the context of an increasingly open international academic community, TOPSOFT scientists are encouraged to become more involved, to follow the results of the world's top academic conferences and share various professional views in the community.

We want to push the science further, together.

Group of Professionals

TOPSOFT INT has members from all over the world across several academic research area.

TSIR Scholars

TOPSOFT INT Research group highly values talents, TSIR Scholars are all strictly selected professionals with great passion.

Non-Commercial

TSIR is not a commercial organisation, nothing is chargeble from the application process to everything that are provided by TSIR.

TSIR Membership

Becoming part of TSIR is a cool thing, not just because you will becoming part of an leading academic community, it would help you on your research journey as well.

TSI Scholars Certification

Upon joining TSIR, you will receive an official membership certificate from TSI and a permanent access to TSI's exclusive academic email of the following form:

yourname@topsoftint.com

Academic Lectures

TOPSOFT international regularly invites industry authorities to open free academic training or lectures to TSI scholars. All TSI research members can freely choose whether to participate.

Conferences Recommendation

TSIR community will have special personnel to weekly push the latest papers, research hotspots, industry news and doctoral research group opportunities of global universities to the community.

The COMMUNITY

Becoming a TSI scholar means that you will become a member of the world's leading academic community. You can share your research views at any time in the community, discuss academic issues with researchers and scholars from all over the world, and obtain top academic contacts.

TSIR Projects

From time to time, TOPSOFT International will crowdsource the development of experimental projects related to the content of the TSI Research Group. Projects that perform well will be handed over to the parent company for commercial piloting and TSI members will receive a share of any subsequent revenue generated. By joining TSI you will have the opportunity to work with You will have the opportunity to work on real projects with researchers from around the world.

Note that: Projects developed by TSI scholars are actually owned by the developers and are subject to software copyright, and can only be handed over to the parent company with the consent of the members.
